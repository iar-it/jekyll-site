Al-Maida Kitchen Help

Islamic Association of Raleigh is seeking a high performing, dependable and hard-working part-time team member/cook at Al-Maida Kitchen.

Being a team member means exhibiting a friendly, enthusiastic attitude while performing job assignments, showing a caring attitude for customers and fellow employees, and maintaining the cafeteria's operational quality and cleanliness standards.

This is a family cafeteria environment where you will be taking customer orders, managing customer payments using a cash register, ensuring dining area and Kitchen are kept clean and meet safety and health standards, preparing and presenting quality products at all times, and maintaining adequately stocked work stations

Job Requirements
  • Prior experience is a preferable, but not required.
  • At least a high school degree
  • Availability to work a flexible schedule including rotational "on-call" for emergencies
  • Proficient in English

Compensation: Compensation is commensurate with experience. The employee will be a non-exempt hourly employee. IAR Personnel policy governs work conditions and benefits.

Please send your resume with a cover letter to jobs@islam1.org with the job code of IAR-Kitchen-Help.
