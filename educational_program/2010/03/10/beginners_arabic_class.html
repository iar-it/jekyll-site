Beginners Arabic Class

Mar 10, 2010

Imam Sameh Asal will be teaching a Beginners Arabic class. The class will be held on Sundays between Maghrib and Isha prayers. This class is free.

The course textbook is Programmed Arabic Islamic Reader. You can purchase the textbook from the IIPH bookstore at Avent Ferry Plaza or order it online from Amazon.com

Starting Time: 6:30 pm
Ending Time: 7:30 pm
Class location: Room 240 (2nd Floor)

If you have any questions, concerns, or comments, please contact Imam Sameh Asal at imamsameh@gmail.com or 834-9572 ext 1357 or contact Musa Lipford at mindpower_inc@msn.com
