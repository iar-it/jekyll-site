Friday Night Program

Schedule of Friday Night Program at the Islamic Center of Raleigh.

The Education Committee offers Friday Night Programs every week in the Prayer Hall of the Islamic Center of Raleigh.

Topics may cover Islamic Jurisprudence, Modern Day Health, Raising Children in the West, and many other topics.

From November to March the program is held after Isha prayers and the rest of the year it is held between Maghrib and Isha prayers.



Short Talk Program

Schedule of Short Talk Program at the Islamic Center of Raleigh.

The Short Talk Program are held at the Islamic Center of Raleigh after daily prayers throughout the week. Presenters present an education topic or an analysis of issues for 5 - 10 minutes.
