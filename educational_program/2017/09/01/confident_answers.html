Confident Answers

Sep 1, 2017

You are bombarded with these questions all the time. What is Sharia law? Are all terrorists Muslim? Why do you dress like that? Are women oppressed in Islam?

These questions are not only challenging in dawah, but they can confuse us and erode at our own faith.

This program will create a safe space where you can not only learn more about these topics, but engage in active training and practice composing your answers while receiving live feedback from your peers and instructors. Come with an open mind and ready to work hard, be challenged, and learn a lot in the process.

Weekly on Tuesdays between Maghrib and Isha prayers
