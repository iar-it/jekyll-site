Al-Ma'idah Kitchen & Café

Nov 8, 2008

Al-Ma'idah Café is a community supported kitchen that serves the Triangle Muslim Community. All proceeds go to support the Islamic Center of Raleigh's many projects and services to the community including its schools and buildings.

Al-Ma'idah Café offers the best in Authentic Middle Eastern, Asian and American Cuisine. We offer dine-in, food to go, and an outstanding catering services.
