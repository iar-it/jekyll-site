Services

Jul 16, 2008

The IAR provides a variety of services for the local Muslim community, including burial services at our Zebulon cemetery, on-site food and catering services through our Al-Ma'idah Kitchen, an Islamic Library, and much more.


Cemetery

Learn more about our Zebulon Cemetery and procedures to follow in case of a death in the family.


Hajj Services

The IAR organizes annual trips to Hajj for any interested parties. Learn more about how to become part of these groups here.


IAR Endowment Fund (Waqf)

Inspired by the historical concept of a Waqf, an inalienable religious endowment, the Islamic Association of Raleigh aims to empower the long term financial health of the greater Raleigh Muslim community and provide halal financing collateral for strategic community development projects.
