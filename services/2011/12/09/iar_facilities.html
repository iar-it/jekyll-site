IAR Facilities

Dec 9, 2011

The masjid is the center of a Muslim life. We encourage our members to attend the five daily prayers, daily and weekly educational and Islamic lectures, Islamic classes, Friday Night program, youth activities and sports activities offered by dedicated group of volunteers.

In order to manage all of the activities, we encourage the various Committee members and Muslim community to use the IAR Facilities reservation system.

Please check the Facilities calendar to find out availability of a room. Next step is to use the reservation form to request the room.

Committee members can use the announcement form to notify community members of their event or activity.

Please remember, first and foremost we are doing these activities to please Allah swt and we should be mindful of Allah's presence in all of our actions and deeds. Use the facilities with care and leave them better than how you found them. If you have to report any damage please contact the IAR Management team.
