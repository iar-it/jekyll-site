Directions to the Cemetery

Aug 15, 2009

The Muslim cemetery is located in Wendell North Carolina approximately 25 miles to the north east of the Islamic Center of Raleigh.

The street address for the cemetery is 6732 Hankinson Rd Wendell, NC 27591, it is located off Edgemont Road in Wendell NC.

How to follow behind the hearse

1 Line up behind the hearse
2 Turn on your headlights
3 Turn on your emergency flashers
4 Stay together as you follow behind the hearse
5 You are permitted to carefully drive through a stop sign or red light while following the hearse
