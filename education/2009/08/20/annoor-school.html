Important News

The shura council at the Islamic Association of Raleigh in its May 9th, 2010 meeting is proud to announce the following:

Approval of the report submitted by the shura appointed evaluation team. The An-Noor School is being moved from the pilot program to Permanent Program.


An-Noor Full Time Quran Memorization School's mission

To Establish an institution that will nurture a strong connection with the Book of Allah, the Glorious Quran, through its memorization, understanding and implementation, and will develop well-rounded future leaders who are guided by the Quran and Sunnah.


School Curriculum and Fees

In addition to the Quran memorization, Students will also receive regular curricular instruction through age-appropriate Math, Science, social study, and English Language Art classes.

An-Noor tuition is $325.00 per month for one child, two or more children-$275 per child per month. Parents can apply for a scholarship or financial assistance through the social and welfare committee if they are going through financial hardship.

Please register your children for this enormous opportunity. The school is now accepting applications on a first come first serve basis.


School Registration

An-Noor Quran Memorization School is now accepting applications for the 2011-2012 academic year. It has developed a unique program, tailored to the Quran Hifz and the academic needs of the students and to meet the NC Standard Course of Study (NCSOS) requirements.

The An-Noor school has opened admissions for grades 3 to 6 for the academic year 2011 - 2012. The students will study Language Arts, Math, and Science to meet the Wake County and the National Achievement Tests' criteria. They also learn Social Studies as an additional subject.


School Details

Students are enrolled for Grades Three to Six only. School runs from Monday to Friday from 7:45 AM to 4 PM, with fours hours of Quran memorization and three hours of PE, Language Arts, Mathematics and Science.

For further information, please contact the An-Noor School administration an-noor@islam1.org or talk to the An-Noor School principal Sr Kariman in person. Her office is located on the second floor, Room 240 and her office hours are from 11:30 AM to 2:00 PM weekdays


An-Noor Quran Program Summer 2011

An-Noor School is organizing two different programs to enhance Quran reading and memorization during the summer holidays. A weekend program and a weekly program to help accommodate the needs of the community. You do not have to be enrolled in the An-Noor School to attend these summer classes, they are open to all, admission is on a first come first serve basis.

Please take advantage of this opportunity and preserve the word of Allah subhanahu wataa'la by memorizing, understanding, and implementing the Holy Quran.
