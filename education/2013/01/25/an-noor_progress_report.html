An-Noor Progress Report

Jan 25, 2013

An-Noor School is pleased to share the news that by the Blessings of Allah eleven students have completed the Hifdh (Memorization) of the Holy Qur'an in the current academic year 2012-2013.

In the 2011-2012 academic year three students had completed their Hifdh-ul-Quran and in the 2010-2011 academic year two students completed their Hifdh-ul-Quran..

This brings the number of students who have completed their Hifdh to sixteen since the inception of the school. Alhamdulillah.

We congratulate all of the students, their parents, the teachers for their hard work and dedication to preserve and protect the word of Allah swt. It is indeed a blessing from Allah swt that the students are able to achieve this honor and distinction of becoming a Hifdh.

We have several new openings because of the success of our students in completing their goals.

An-Noor School Admission Policy

Our admission policy is based on first come, first serve basis, therefore we encourage all interested parents to contact the School Director on the 2nd floor of IAR between the hours of 8:00 AM and 12:00 noon on weekdays to pre-register for the next academic year.

Student eligibility for admission to An-Noor School is determined through an interview process that tests their Quran reading skills and report card from their current school.
