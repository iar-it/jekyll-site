An-Noor Summer Program

Jun 3, 2011

Learn Quran during Summer Holidays

An-Noor School is organizing two different programs to enhance Quran reading and memorization during the summer holidays. A weekend program and a weekly program to help accommodate the needs of the community. You do not have to be enrolled in the An-Noor School to attend these summer classes, they are open to all, admission is on a first come first serve basis.

Please take advantage of this opportunity and preserve the word of Allah subhanahu wataa'la by memorizing, understanding, and implementing the Holy Quran.


An-Noor Summer Weekend Program

The An-Noor Summer Weekend session will be held from June 25 through August 19 2011. The classes will be held from 10 AM to 12 Noon on Saturdays and Sundays.

Admission will be granted on first come first serve basis and is open to all, you do not have to be enrolled in An-Noor school. The applicant should be 6 years or older. There is no qualification criterion to join this program.

Tuition is $200.00 and registration deadline is June 25, 2011


An-Noor Summer Weekly Program

The An-Noor Summer Weekly session is also open to all. This program is also geared for students who plan to enroll in the full time An-Noor program in the fall semester.

The An-Noor Summer Weekend session will be held from June 27 through August 19 2011.The classes will held from 8 AM to Noon Monday to Friday.

Tuition is $350 (2 months) and the registration deadline is June 27, 2011


Qualification Criteria

  • All students with some Quran reading fluency can be admitted into this program.
  • All students will be evaluated for the Quran reading competency.
  • If we do not have enough enrollments to support this program, we will cancel the program and return the fees


Summer Student Registration Form

Please download and print the registration form and and bring it to An-Noor School Office located on the 2nd floor of the Masjid on Saturdays and Sundays between 8 AM to 11 AM.


Contact Details
