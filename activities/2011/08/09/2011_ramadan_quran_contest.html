2011 Ramadan Quran Contest

Aug 9, 2011

An-Noor Quran Academy is proud to announce the 2011 Ramadan Quran contest. The contest is encouraging all Muslims to learn and memorize Quran using proper rules of tajweed.

Rules

  1. A contestant can register in only one level
  2. Registration form can be filled online or dropped at the registration table in the lobby at IAR. The registration deadline is September 6th 2011.
  3. The registration fee is $15.00 (nonrefundable) per contestant. The registration fee should be paid on the day of contest.
  4. Age is calculated from date of birth until September 24, 2011.
  5. Boys and girls will not be judged separately, there will be at least one female judge in all categories.
  6. Incorrect information on Date of Birth may disqualify the contestant.
  7. Younger contestants can compete in higher level according to their abilities.
  8. During testing, contestant may be asked to recite any part(s) of the Surahs that he/she memorized including the bonus
  9. Grade of the bonus Surah would be considered to break a tie.
  10. Performance is based on Memorization, Tarteel (rules of Tajweed) and Pronunciation.
  11. Following Grading criteria will be followed
    • First Place 85% - 100%
    • Second Place 70% - 84%
    • Third Place 60% - 69%
  12. Top scorers will get the 1st, 2nd and 3rd places i.e. all three contestants can achieve scores in the range of 85% - 100%



Important Dates

  • Application deadline: September 6th 2011 (Registration is now closed)
  • Contest Dates: September 24 2011 for categories B1 - B6 from 10 AM to 5 PM
  • Contest Dates: October 1 2011 for categories I1 - I6 and A1 - A4 from 10 AM to 5 PM
  • Contest Dates: October 8 2011 for categories V1 - V5 from 10 AM to 5 PM
  • Quran Contest Ceremony: October 28 2011

Every registered participant should have received the Quran Contest Schedule based on their selected category. Please send email to quran-contest@islam1.org if you have any questions or have not yet received any email about the Quran Contest.



Contact

For more information or questions, please contact quran-contest@islam1.org
