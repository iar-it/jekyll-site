Middle School Girls Summer Day Camp

May 21, 2011

MYC 2011 Summer Day Camp for Girls in 3rd through 8th Grade


The camp will be held on Friday July 8, 2011 from 9 am - 6 pm at the Camp Durant Nature Park and at the YMCA.

Register early! Space is limited to the first 45 applicants. Girls must be 8 years old by July 1, 2011 to apply.

Cost for attending the camp is $25.00 with early registration now through June 17. Transportation, Camp t-shirt, lunch and snacks included in the cost of camp.

Late registration is $35 from June 18 to June 30. Registration closes promptly on June 30.

Important Information for Summer Camp

  • All drop off and pick up will be at the Islamic Center of Raleigh.
  • The bus will leave promptly at 9:00 AM.
  • Morning activities at Camp Durant Nature Park are subject to change due to weather conditions.
  • Transportation, Camp t-shirt, lunch and snacks included.
  • Apply sunscreen prior to arrival and send extra sunscreen to reapply during the day.
  • Strongly recommend wearing a hat for outdoor activities.
  • Send a backpack with your child with her personal items labeled.
  • Bring a towel, bathing suit, and flip flops/sandals for activities at the swimming pool.
  • Wear sneakers.
  • Bring appropriate attire for prayer.
  • Do not send any electronics gadgets or electronic games. Your child will be responsible for ensuring safety of electronic gadgets.
  • Lunch, water, and snacks will be provided. Kids get hungry so please pack extra snacks at your discretion.
