Women's Committee Goals and Members

Mar 23, 2011

Mission and Vision
  1. To strive to enable and promote sisterhood and unity among sisters irrespective of their ethnic, nationality, social or cultural backgrounds.
  2. To strive to enable and facilitate learning opportunities for sisters to strengthen their Islamic knowledge and values.
  3. To strive to instill Islamic and social environment to foster Islamic values, conduct, respect and love for each other.
