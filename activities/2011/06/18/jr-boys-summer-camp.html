IAR's 2013 MYC Elementary / Middle School Boys' Summer Camp

Jun 18, 2011

Registration for the IAR's 2013 Muslim Youth Coalition (MYC) Elementary/Middle School Boys' Summer Camp is now open!

The camp will be held from Thursday July 4 2013 through Sunday July 7 2013 at Jordan Lake State Recreation Area.

Attendance to the Summer camp is restricted to boys 8-14 years old.


Activities

We have planned many activities, including talks/reflection about Allaah, swimming, field sports, (saheeh/authentic) stories, water sports, obstacle courses, wrestling, capture the flag, water balloon games, dodgeball, archery, team-building games, skills training, hiking, bonfires, skits, smores, water-tag, interactive discussions, and, of course, water-tag!


Registration

The registration fees includes cost of travel, food, activities and lodging.

EARLY Registration ($60 per person)*: Friday, March 31 - Thursday, June 6

NORMAL Registration ($70 per person)*: Friday, June 7 - Thursday, June 20

LATE Registration ($80 per person)*: Friday, June 21 - Friday, June 28

* Please remember to inquire about our multiple children discount.

Space is LIMITED, so please sign up ASAP! Priority is given to regular participants of the IAR MYC's Elementary/Middle School Boys' Saturday Program.

Please register for the camp using this 2013 Summer Camp Registration Form.


Contact

For more details, please contact Br Mahen Khan via email mahenmkhan@gmail.com or 919-757-1979 [call/text]. You can also contact Br Muammar Dahnoun or Br Aatif Abdul-Qadeer.

If you have any questions, comments, concerns, suggestions, naseehah (advice), please rush to contact us, for the sake of pleasing Allaah 'azza wa jalla, alone!

We are looking forward to an amazing time at the camp, inn shaa'a Allaah. A goal of ours is to help develop legendary youth that are closest to Allaah on the day of judgment. Because this is a community effort, we appreciate the help of each parent that is seriously involved in this work for their kids, particularly through role-modeling of behaviors, community service and volunteerism, seeking/teaching of Islaamic education, and the support of those that are working with their kids.

Baaraka Allaahu feekum wa rafa'a Allaahu darajatakum fee al mutatahhireena al mukhlaseena al muqarrabeena al mahbuubeena al muhsineena al muttaqeena al abraari! Ahsana Allaahu ilaikum.
