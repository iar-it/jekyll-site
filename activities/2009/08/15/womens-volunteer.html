Volunteer Opportunities

Aug 15, 2009

Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said, "The best one among you, is he who is most useful to his community." (Narrated by Bukhari and Muslim).

The IAR Sisters' Committee would like to invite you to volunteer for different activities that it undertakes. Dedicated, dependable and passionate volunteers are the key for success of any volunteer organization.

Volunteer Opportunities include the following:

  • Volunteer to organize for Eid prayers
  • Supervision and help during Friday Jumuah prayers (3 shifts)
  • Supervision and help during Taraweeh Prayers
  • Baby Sitting during Friday Night Program
  • Baby Sitting during Jummah Prayers
  • Baby Sitting during Taraweh prayers

Below projects / activities will be undertaken, provided we have sufficient volunteers:

  • Beautification and House Keeping of Sisters' musallah area and Play room
  • Support and resources for New Muslimahs / Revert Musalimahs
  • Sisters' Workshops and Socializing
  • Parents of the Blessed
  • Health and Fitness

Please note that IAR Sisters' Committee operates within the bounds and guidelines defined by IAR Shura and Executive Committee, without stepping over the areas of responsibilities of the other executive committees.

The IAR Sisters' Committee needs your support to successfully carry on its activities or to undertake new projects that are beneficial to the sisters. If you are interested, please send email to iar.sisters.committee@gmail.com.

Please specify your skills, availability and interests, we can assign you with the right group, so you can thrive to success and find volunteering very satisfying.
