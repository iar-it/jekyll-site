NEW! IAR Girls Basketball League

Islamic Association of Raleigh Girl's Basketball League was founded in 2005 to provide Muslim girls ages 8-18 a positive recreational activity bringing sisterhood and sportsmanship together, while maintaining an Islamic way of life. We have revamped this program into a 3x3, 5x5 basketball league to enhance player development, teamwork and knowledge of the sport. The "Queen of the Court" Mini-tournaments will be played every 1st Saturday 5:30 pm - 8:00 pm starting February 3, 2018. IARGBL Season will run as follows: WINTER JAN.-FEB. & SPRING MAR.-APR. 2018
Team advisers and volunteers have assisted in making this league successful and we are always looking for more dedicated sisters to join our mission supporting the girls in our community and beyond.
