IAR Older Adult Committee (OAC) Program

"Connecting generations to build a better future"

As Salaamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullahi wa Barakatahu

Brothers and Sisters,
Thank you for visiting the IAR Older Adult/ Men and Women Committee webpage. We are dedicated to enhancing the lives of seniors by using local resources and/or outreach services that possibly enhance the quality of life for members at IAR. We are a volunteer activity group guided by the teachings of Qur'an and Sunnah.

Should you, of any age, have a passion, interest in sharing your skills, crafts and expertise with older adults or find you need support, please, join us.

Mission

Our mission is to encourage senior adults to collaborate and exchange their skills, talents and strengths with youth and adults in the community.

OAC Activities
(activities undertaken provided we have sufficient volunteers)
  • Monthly Resource Meetings
  • Annual Events (Iftars)
  • Conduct Seminars and Workshops
  • Annual fund-raising
  • Ongoing OAC support network drive

If you have any suggestions, send email to iar.senior@gmail.com. Kindly specify how you would like to improve a specific area or concern and whether you can volunteer and be part of that improvement process.
