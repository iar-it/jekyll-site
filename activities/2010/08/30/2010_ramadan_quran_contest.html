2010 Ramadan Quran Contest

Aug 30, 2010

An-Noor Quran Academy is proud to announce the Ramadan Quran contest. The contest is encouraging all Muslims to learn and memorize Quran using proper rules of tajweed.

Registration is closed.

Rules

  1. A contestant can register in only one level
  2. Registration form can be filled online or dropped at the registration table in the lobby at IAR. The registration deadline is October 3rd 2010.
  3. The registration fee is $15.00 (nonrefundable) per contestant. The registration fee should be paid on the day of contest.
  4. Age is calculated from date of birth until October 9, 2010.
  5. For levels C to J, Boys and Girls will be judged separately.
  6. Incorrect information on Date of Birth may disqualify the contestant.
  7. Younger contestants can compete in higher level according to their abilities.
  8. During testing, contestant may be asked to recite any part(s) of the Surahs that he/she memorized including the bonus
  9. Grade of the bonus Surah would be considered to break a tie.

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: September 26 2010
  • Contest Dates: October 9, October 16 and October 23
  • Award Ceremony: November 19 2010

Notes

  1. Contest date for specific levels will be decided after the registration dead line.
  2. Contest schedule will be posted on the IAR website and an email notification will be sent to all the applicants.
  3. During testing, contestant may be asked to recite any part(s) of the main Surah's and bonus Surah that he/she memorized.
  4. Performance is based on Memorization, Tarteel (rules of Tajweed) and Pronunciation.
