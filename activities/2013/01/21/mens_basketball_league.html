Men's Basketball League

Jan 21, 2013

The IAR Sports Committee is once again proud to sponsor the Fall 2013 Men's (over 30) Basketball League.

Registration period is from August 17-24

In order to register:
(1) must be over age 30
(2) complete application information
(3) sign liability waiver form from IAR*
(4) pay registration fee ($20) to cover jersey cost **
* please print and bring during first week of play
** applicable only to new participants or for those who've lost jerseys (plan on using last seasons reversible mesh jersey). Please bring cash payment during first week of play.

Games will be played every Sunday in the IAR gym between Asr and Isha prayers.

Tentative dates for season are Sept 1st through December 21st.

Plan to InshaAllah start immediately after completion of the Asr prayer. Break for Magrib prayer. Pending participants and schedule, time available between Magrib and Isha prayers for further games.

Pending numbers of participants, formal teams and schedules may be made.

Pending numbers of participants, games may be full court (5 versus 5) , or two half court (4 versus 4) games simultaneously.

Last season, an email would go out a few days prior to game day to see who is available for the week to play.

Once final numbers for participants are determined, more information on teams and style of play will be sent out.
