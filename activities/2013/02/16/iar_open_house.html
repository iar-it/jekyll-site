IAR Open House 2016: A Bridge to Understanding Islam

Feb 16, 2013

IAR is hosting it's annual Open House event Saturday March 5th from 12pm to 4pm at the IAR, featuring Imam AbuTaleb and guest speaker Dr. Carl Earnst from UNC- Chapel Deptartment of Religious Studies. Come learn about Islam, Muslim culture, and sample culinary treats from around the world.

Date: Saturday March 5th 2016
Time: 12 PM - 4 PM

Location:
Islamic Association of Raleigh
808 Atwater Street
Raleigh, NC 27607

Contact: outreach@islam1.org for more info.

Itinerary:
12:00pmArrival and Registration in the main lobby
12:00-1:15Visit various booths in the gym to learn about the mosque and various aspects of the Muslim community, Islam, art. Culinary treats from around the Muslim world will be provided
1:15-1:25Hear the call to prayer and assemble in the prayer hall
1:25-1:35Observe the noon prayer
1:35-1:45Quran Recitation by An-Noor Students
1:45-1:5599 Names of Allah song by Al-Iman students
1:55-2:15What is Islam? by Fiaz Fareed
2:15-2:40Islamic Heritage by Al-Iman students
2:40-3:00Lecture by Imam AbuTaleb
3:00-3:20Lecture by Dr. Carl Earnst, UNC-Chapel Hill
3:20-4:00Q&A with Imam AbuTaleb and Dr. Carl Earnst
