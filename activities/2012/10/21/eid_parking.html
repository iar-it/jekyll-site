Eid Parking

Oct 21, 2012

Since parking at Islamic Center of Raleigh is very limited, we are seeking your cooperation and asking you to comply with the following instructions.

  • Due to limited parking spaces, carpooling among family members and friends is strongly encouraged. Please carpool.
  • Please follow the instructions of Traffic Controllers, Police, and IAR Security Personnel.
  • Additional offsite parking is provided using the K-Mart Parking lot on Blue Ridge Road.
  • Please Drop Off and Pick Up your family members and friends at designated areas on Atwater Street and Ligon Street as shown on the attached map and as directed by Traffic Controllers and Security Personnel.
  • After Drop Off, drive your car to Blue Ridge Road using Eid Traffic directions; park your car on the K-Mart parking lot on Blue Ridge Road. The Taxis/Minivan shuttle service between the ICR and the K-Mart parking lot is provided by IAR; it is free and will be available every 15 minutes.
  • Please use Method Rd to come to the ICR. Drop off your family members and friends at Atwater Street Drop Off/Pick Up site. After drop off please park your car at the K-Mart parking lot.
  • After Eid prayer, please come to your Drop Off/Pick Up site and take the shuttle to pick up your car from the K-Mart parking lot.
  • In order to avoid creating traffic jams and delays, please be considerate and use minimal time to drop off and pick up your family members and friends. Please coordinate in advance with your family members and friends that they should be waiting for you at the pick-up site before your arrival; you should not wait for them.
  • After Eid prayer please remove your car from the K-Mart parking lot as soon as possible.
