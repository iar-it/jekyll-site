Annual Open House

The annual Open House event is organized by the IAR Daawah Committee. We invite all of our non-Muslim neighbors, friends, colleagues to come and visit the Islamic Center of Raleigh.

Annual IAR Picnic

The Islamic Association of Raleigh hosts an annual picnic in which the entire community is invited to enjoy sports, free food and a chance to meet other Muslims!

Community Health Fair

The Islamic Association of Raleigh and the local health care community put together an annual free Community Health Fair which provides free checkups and medical information for the entire community.

Community Events

The Islamic Association of Raleigh has planned the following Community Events for 2017. The goal of these events is to foster brotherhood and increase participation in various activities.

Most of the events repeat annually and are well received and attended by the community members, neighbors, and guests. Please plan to attend these events and invite your neighbors and co-workers for the IAR Open House.
