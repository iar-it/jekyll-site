Nov 8, 2008

Girls' Saturday Program

Main coordinator: Sr. Farrah
The Saturday girls' Youth Program is divided into three groups:
Crystals (Grades 1-4)
Jewels (Grades 5-8)
Gems (Grades 9 & above)

The program offers activities and guidance for our young Muslimahs. We offer Islamic discussions, crafts, games, food activities, sports, art activities, field trips, and more!

We are pleased to announce that participation is FREE!

Completion of an online registration form is required. Registration Form

For more information, or if you have questions, please email Sr. Farrah (farrah.khan@iaryouth.org)

Girls' Friday Night Program

Main coordinator: Sr. Walaa

The IAR youth committee offers a program for middle and high school girls which includes various interactive activities, including DIYs, crafts, experiments, food nights, and games. The program is designed to create a welcoming and comfortable environment for Muslim girls to spend time with each other, have fun, and build close friendships within their community.

  • When: Friday nights, after Maghreb salah
  • Where: IAR 2nd floor, room 240

For more information, or if you have questions, please email Sr. Walaa (wsarsou@ncsu.edu)
