Ramadan Essay Contest

May 17, 2018

Introduction: This is an individual essay contest that you will submit within a specified date. You will be judged based on your depth of content, authenticity, research, a clear and coherent understanding of the material, writing style, and answering the questions. There will be three prize winners, a 1st place prize, a 2nd place prize, and a 3rd place prize. There will be two different divisions, but the topics are the same. One division is for college contestants, and the other is for high school.

Topic: Pick one single verse in the Quran that you really felt attached to and write about it (please only pick one verse). Explain the meaning of the verse, why you chose it, why you felt that this specific verse was important, and how it applies to your daily life and the life of the society at large.

Sources: The Quran and Sunnah are two obvious sources. Other sources you may use include journal articles, news articles, books or lectures/videos. Please cite your sources as plagiarism is unacceptable.

1st Place Prize: $200

2nd Place Prize: $100

3rd Place Prize: $70

Minimum requirements: College essays: minimum 1,000 words. High school essays: minimum 750 words. Essays that are well written and have depth will be considered regardless if they meet the minimum word count.

Submission dates: Essay submissions begin on May 16th and end on June 6th of 2018. In order to be considered as a potential prize winner: please make sure your first and last name is on your essay. Also on your essay, please write your age, your school name (high school or college), and how we may contact you (either email or phone so we may contact you with comments and to inform you whether you won).

If you have any questions or want to submit your essay, please email ramadanessaycontest@gmail.com The winners for both high school and college categories will be announced on Eid. We look forward to reading your essays and hope that you enjoy writing it.

JazakumAllahu kheiran
