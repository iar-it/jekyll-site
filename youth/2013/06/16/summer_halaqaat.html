IAR MYC Summer Intensive Halaqaat Series

Jun 16, 2013

This series is to help keep our youth motivated and connected to Allaah. When most of our youth are out of school, they will still be learning our Deen and its embodiment, inn shaa'a Allaah. After all, our Deen is our life, and Allaah honors us and raises legendary individuals through this Deen.

Overview

Gender: Male / Female (separate halaqaat for comfort and Allaah's pleasure, inn shaa'a Allaah)
 
Age Group: 7-18, though we hold the right to circumstantially rescind enrollment
 
Topics: Names/Attributes of Allaah, 'azza wa jalla; Minor/Major Signs of the Day of Judgment; Day of Judgment; Descriptions of Jannah/Jahannam; Sharee'ah and Global Justice, Service, etc.
 
Cost: For calling to Allaah, $0
 
Times/Days: 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM, Mondays and Wednesdays
 
Dates: June 10, 2013 until July 3, 2013
