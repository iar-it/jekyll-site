The Islamic Center of Raleigh

We are located at 808 Atwater Street, Raleigh NC 27607. You can reach us by phone at 919 834-9572 or by email at office@islam1.org

We are nestled between NC State University campus and I-440. Parking during Friday prayers is very limited. To alleviate the parking problem we offer three shifts of Friday prayers. It is highly recommended to car pool during Friday prayers. NC State bus shuttle stops right at our door.

The Islamic Center of Raleigh has two buildings. The red brick building facing Ligon Street and the newer white building facing Atwater Street. The prayer services and events are held in the new white building, also known as the masjid.

You can enter the main building from either Ligon Street or Atwater Street. The Multipurpose Hall / Gym, the Library and the IAR Offices are located on the first floor of the masjid. Women's Prayer Hall is on the second floor of the masjid. The doors for the masjid are open daily for the five prayers.

Al Iman School, An-Noor Quran Academy, and Al Furqan School are located in the main building and the red brick building.

The masjid building also has a cafeteria "Al Ma'idah Kitchen" which serves Middle Eastern lunch and dinner daily for the community members. The Al Ma'idah kitchen is located on the right hand corner as you enter the premises from Atwater Street.

Driving Directions

Directions from Cary or North Raleigh

  1. From I-440 take exit 2A Western Boulevard towards NC State University
  2. At the first traffic light take a left on Method Road
  3. After 0.3 miles take a right on Ligon Street
  4. After 0.1 miles take a left on Atwater Street
  5. Turn left into the masjid parking lot

Directions from RDU, RTP, or Durham

  1. From I-40 East towards Raleigh
  2. Take Wade Avenue Exit on I-40
  3. After 4 miles turn right on Faircloth Street
  4. Faircloth Street becomes Gorman Street after the first traffic light
  5. Continue on Gorman Street
  6. Turn right on the first traffic light on Ligon Street
  7. After 0.3 miles turn right on Atwater Street
  8. Turn left into the masjid parking lot



Cemetery

The Muslim cemetery is located in Wendell North Carolina approximately 25 miles to the north east of the Islamic Center of Raleigh. The street address for the cemetery is Hankinson Rd Wendell, NC 27591, it is located off Edgemont Road in Wendell.
