Qiyam (Night Prayers) and Itekaf for Last 10 Nights

May 23, 2019

The last 10 nights of Ramadan will start on Saturday overnight into Sunday, May 25-26. There will be nightly qiyam prayers starting at 3:15am and lasting approximately half hour plus witr, led by Sh. Hoosain Dalvi and guest reciters.

Those wishing to make itekaf must register with the Security office and familiarize themselves with the guidelines. Suhoor (pre-dawn meal) will be provided by IAR.

Kindly note that no unsupervised children will be allowed overnight without parental supervision.
