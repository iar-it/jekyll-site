Summer Day Camp

Jul 5, 2019

THRIVE is proud to announce our 2019 summer day camp.

Our theme this summer is VOYAGE: A Journey to Excellence. Our goal is to help Muslim children develop a love of Islam through a unique and well-rounded learning experience.

We hope to help our children strengthen their Muslim identity through knowledge, community, and fun. 

THE PROGRAM

Through the camp experience, we hope to: 

  • Inspire a sense of pride in each child's identity as a Muslim
  • Build companionship & community
  • Solidify their basic understanding of Islam 
  • Develop a culture of excellence

We will do this using the following topics:

  • Foundations of Faith: 
    • 6 pillars of Iman - each week is dedicated to 1 pillar
  • STEM
  • Health & Wellness
  • Language & Performing Arts

THE DISCOUNT

Currently, we have over 40 spots still open! With so much room for new campers to benefit from such a great program, we have decided to reduce the rate for any families starting on our second week of the camp!
