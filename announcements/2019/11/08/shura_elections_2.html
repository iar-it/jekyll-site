Shura Elections

Nov 8, 2019

IAR Shura Elections will be held on Sunday November 17 from 10:00 am to 2:30 pm. The voting will take place at the IAR Library. Please bring a valid picture ID.

The Friday Night Program on November 15 after Isha prayers will be an opportunity for the Shura candidates to introduce themselves.

The final voting and shura-eligible members lists are posted on the bulletin boards.

If you have any questions, please email membership@islam1.org.
