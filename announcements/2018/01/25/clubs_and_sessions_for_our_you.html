Clubs and Sessions for Our Youth

Jan 25, 2018

The Youth Committee at the IAR is offering a wide variety of Clubs & Sessions for our youth. So far we have Adhan & Iqamah Club, Science Club, Technology Club and career club. Generally all clubs and sessions require registration and are 6 weeks long from 6:30pm to 8:00pm with breaks for Salah. Youth will have the opportunity to network with other youth and with our professional community members while gaining a skill. Our goal is to offer more Clubs & Sessions as they become available. For more information and to register please see below.
Adhan & Iqamah Club:
Boys will learn how to make the Adhan & Iqama. If certified they will be allowed to make the Adhan and Iqama at the IAR.
Science Club:
Will engage middle school boys & girls with a number of exciting experiments in three areas of science: Physics, Chemistry and biology.
Technology Club:
Will engage Middle School girls with practical guide to the basics of coding, web design and computer software. The goal is to help spark passion for the technology and software design.
Career:
For high school and college boys and girls. Six-week intensive will give students all they need to know to be career focused. We will discuss various strategies that will give students the edge they need when it comes to preparing for a career. This includes everything from setting up a resume to preparing for an interview.
Technology Club for Boys
engage Middle School boys with practical guide to the basics of coding, web design and computer software. The goal is to help spark passion for the technology and software design.
