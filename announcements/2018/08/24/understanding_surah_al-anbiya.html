Understanding Surah Al-Anbiya

Aug 24, 2018

The Friday Night Program for August 24 after Maghrib prayers is titled "Understanding Surah Al-Anbiya".

The Quran Recitation of the Surah al-Anbiya will be by Sr Ayeshah Traore. Presentations of the surah will be by Abdushakure Ali, Imran Akhtar, Hasaan Mahmood, and Mairah Khan. The Dua will be by Sr Yusrah Oyewole. The host for the event will be Sufyan Abdul Latif.

Uthman bin Affan reported: The Prophet, peace and blessings be upon him, said, "The best of you are those who learn the Quran and teach it." Recorded in Sahih Bukhari.
