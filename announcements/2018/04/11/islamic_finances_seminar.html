Islamic Finances Seminar

Apr 11, 2018

IAR is delighted to announce our first Islamic Finances Seminar for our community. The seminar will be held on Saturday April 21 from 11 am - 4:30 pm in the Main Prayer Hall. The seminar is free for the community and all are welcome to join and learn!

The topics covered in the seminar will be Zakah Calculations, Contracts & Loans, Financial Wellness, Estate Planning & Wills.

The speakers will be Ahmad Quqa, Nada Mohammed, Imam Muamar Dahnoun, Imam Mohamed AbuTaleb, and Nizar Mashal.

Program starts at 11 am

11 am - 12 pm Ahmad Quqa - Financial Wellness
12 pm - 1 pm Sr Nada Muhamed - Wills & Estate Planning

Break For Lunch and Salah

2 pm - 3 pm Imam Mohamed AbuTaleb - Zakat Calculations
3 pm - 4 pm Imam Muamar Dahnoun - Islamic Contracts and Transactions
4 pm - 4:30 pm Nizar Mashal - Shari'a aspect of financial dealings
