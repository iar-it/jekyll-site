"Jummah prayer announcement" Update Sep 14th

Sep 14, 2018

The IAR will hold two shifts for Jumuah (Friday) prayers at 11:30am and 1:00pm. The 3rd shift will NOT be held due to the inclement weather. No activities except prayers are being held at the IAR facilities. Please monitor our website www.raleighmasjid.org for notification on reopening.

Please be advised that IAR decides based on conditions in the immediate vicinity. Members should exercise their discretion on their safety in coming to the center, particularly those with medical conditions, the elderly, or those coming from a distance. Those who find excessive hardship in coming may pray in their homes based on the rukhsa (license) given by our Prophet (saw) for such conditions.

Anyone unable to attend Jumuah prayers should pray Dhuhr 4 raka'at in their homes.

For any questions or concerns, please contact IAR by email (security@islam1.org) or by phone 919-357-3811
