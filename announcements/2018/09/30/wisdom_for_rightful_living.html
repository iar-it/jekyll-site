Wisdom for Rightful Living

Sep 30, 2018

The Living Islam Series of classes taught by Imam Abutaleb and Imam Muamar attempts to contextualize the Prophetic guidance in our contemporary lives through the Forty Hadith of Imam Nawawi.

The goal of the Wisdom for Rightful Living class is to seek closeness to Allah through the understanding of central principles of faith, law, worship, life, and spirituality.

All are welcome and we have diverse attendance from high schoolers to seniors, and we especially encourage college students and young professionals to attend and benefit.

The series continues every Saturday from 8 - 10 AM at IAR on the Second Floor Prayer Hall. Please bring a notebook or a device to take notes, a physical or electronic copy of the Quran (with translation if needed), and a mind ready to think critically and learn.
